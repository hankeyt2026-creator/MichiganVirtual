Meet the Cactus Crew

Sage the Saguaro

Sage is the wise elder of the cactus crew. Towering and calm, Sage has been soaking up sunshine for decades and always knows how to stay grounded.

Poppy the Prickly Pear

Poppy is bright, cheerful, and full of personality. With colorful pads and a flair for drama, Poppy brings energy to the whole team.

Barlow the Barrel Cactus

Short, round, and surprisingly tough, Barlow is the dependable one. He may look cute, but he’s built to survive anything the desert throws at him.

Bella the Bunny Ear

Bella is the soft-looking but spicy member of the crew. Her adorable ear-shaped pads hide tiny glochids that keep everyone respectful of her space.
